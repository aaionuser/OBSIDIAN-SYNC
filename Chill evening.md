##### Connecting require energy
How many of us are low energy people? The truth is that we possess energy, its just that the energy is just reserved within ourselves and they are not evident on the surface. Unfortunately, that energy is needed cause eventually connecting with people require energy. You need to be intentional about it.

##### Take action to do everything I could to build connection
1. Searching for reminders of our time together
   "For example, the picture of us together, people love finding themselves in the pictures"
2. Working to remember their names
3. Trying to make them feel special
4. Making my visit personal for as many people as possible
   "Give them copies of items such as souvenier from special moments, it act as a reminder of the good ol days"
5. Going out of my way to spend extra time with people
   "Be the last one, dont think about going home early, meet with people"
6. Sharing my mistakes during the sermon
   "If you want people impressed, talk about your success, if you want to be identify, talk about failures"
7. Acknowledging them as a part of my success

One of highly succesful people have in common is the technique of "execution". You won't believe how impactful that is towards achieveing your goals.

The five steps towards execution:
1. Vision
You gotta have clarity on what you want to achieve in your life, one professor in one of this university in the west literally wrote his goals for 8 times in a matter of day, its tell you that you need to have goal or vision to dictate your life. As a result, what you can do it to have a vision first on what your life is going to be by writing up clear goals.

2. Passion
You can think of passion as you are trying to get something e.g you wanted to really ride a horse, but you dont have money to get a ride, since you really wanted to ride a horse, ended up you went to the stable and literally ask the owner to work there just so you can ride a horse, beside, you don't really care how much hours they tell you to work such as 7 hours straight by cleaning the stable.. Even tho you think that it was a good deal that you can get a ride, but its probably worth more to the owner/farmer..

3. Action
This is when you already have clarity in mind, and you know that you this is the thing that force you drive then you gotta take action on it, doing it, take the first step, even if you think that you are not going to get it, remember that you are passionate about it, you dont care the end result.

4. Perserverance
Once the action have been executed, you are now going to keep the momentum going for longer, keep doing the action, keep reapeating the action, even if you get a rejection, you still up for game, renew your method, just so that you can keep your vision alive.

5. Relationship
Finally, Afterall of that hardwork, you need to make a connection, be friend with the person that actually uplift your spirit and motivation, get along with everyone that have positive vibe, if you have people that demotivate you, remove them from your life as it might degrade your potential.
